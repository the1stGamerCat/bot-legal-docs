Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21st 2025


1. Data collected

We collect Diacord Server ID's, User ID's, and Channel ID's; Apex Legends usernames, player ID's, nucleus hashes, and any other optionally entered data.


2. Why collect data?

All data collected is to allow the bot to carry out its functions, user identification, command functionality, and statistics.


3. Data protection

Most data stored has limited protection, that being said, no sensitive data is stored locally. Optional Admin token storage will take place on a third-party server. Data is stored within JSON files on a computer reserved for hosting GaAmer_Bot and an occasional Minecraft server.


4. Is your information shared?

No information is sold to or given to third parties as its purpose is only for user identification, command functionality, and statistics. Optionally entered admin tokens may be stored on a third-party server for safety purposes, due to limited encryption and protection for locally stored data.


5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and we will post the updated policy here. Before and after a policy change is made, it will be announced in our support server alongside the custom status of GaAmer_Bot indicating such. Your continued use of GaAmer_Bot after changes are made to this policy indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.


6. Questions and deletion requests

If you have any questions or a request to delete your data, contact us in our support server.
